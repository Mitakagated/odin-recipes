Stew with lentils and chickpeas

Description

A vegan stew with lentils and chickpeas that captivates with its aroma and rich taste

Ingredients

  • 250g lentils
  • 250g chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1/2 bunch parsley
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • savory
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • oil
