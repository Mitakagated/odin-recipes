Stew with lentils and chickpeas

Description

A vegan stew with lentils and chickpeas that captivates with its aroma and rich taste

Ingredients

  • 250g lentils
  • 250g chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 red pepper
  • 1/2 bunch parsley
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • salt
  • pepper
  • savory
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • oil

Steps

  1. Since lentils and chickpeas have different cooking times, it is good to cook them separately, but without boiling them.
  2. Fry finely chopped onion, garlic, carrot and pepper in a little fat. Add the vegetables and enough water and let them boil for 5-10 minutes.
  3. Mix the flour (preferably toasted) in a little water and add to the stew. Season with salt, black pepper, savory, cumin and red pepper, and instead of ordinary you can put hot or smoked ones.
  4. Leave the dish on the fire for another 10-15 minutes. When serving, sprinkle with finely chopped parsley.
  5. This stew is perfect for cooking in a slow cooker. In this case, we put all the products (without spices and flour) at the beginning of cooking.
  6. When the beans are ready, thicken with flour and season.
