Swirled Banitsa

Swirled Bulgarian Banitsa

A classic Swirled Banitsa! A recipe for a fluffy, soft and very tasty Banitsa with cheese, served with delight to the whole family!

Ingredients

For the Stuffing

  • 4 eggs
  • 100-200g cheese
  • 1 teacup yogurt
  • 1/2 - 1 teacup oil

Universal Pastry and Pizza Dough

  • 1L lukewarm water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cube of fresh yeast
  • around 1/2 teacup oil
  • to 2kg flour (as long as it takes to roll the dough)
